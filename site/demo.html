Try it

A simple demo story for hotel managers.

Start with sample data. Show useful answers. Then show that live changes need approval.

Start Availability Today Pricing Approval

1. Start with sample hotel data

The demo works even without real Resly credentials, so the story is safe to show.

resly --json doctor

2. Answer a guest question

"How many rooms are available for 3 people this weekend?"

resly --json room-types list
resly --json inventory get --room-type 1BR-GARDEN --from 2026-07-05 --to 2026-07-07
resly --json rates get --rate-plan BAR-1BR-GARDEN --from 2026-07-05 --to 2026-07-07

3. Show the morning brief

Arrivals, departures, booking changes, and room pressure in one place.

resly --json reservations list --from 2026-07-01 --to 2026-07-31 --limit 10
resly --json reservations in-house
resly --json blocks list --date-type in-between --from 2026-07-01 --to 2026-07-07

4. Prepare a price change

The assistant can prepare a rate change preview. Nothing is published yet.

resly --json rates preview --rate-plan BAR-1BR-GARDEN --file examples/rate-update.json

5. Show the safety lock

A live change needs a separate approval token. Without it, the change is refused.

resly --json rates update --rate-plan BAR-1BR-GARDEN --file examples/rate-update.json --live

What Resly is selling

A hotel-friendly way to use Resly data: faster manager answers, safer pricing workflows, fewer repetitive checks, and chat-based help that does not get unsafe powers.
