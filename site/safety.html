Safe changes

The assistant can help. It cannot quietly change the hotel.

Managers stay in control of anything that affects live bookings, prices, rooms, or alerts.

Simple rule

Look first. Approve second. Change last.

Claude or Codex can check the hotel data and prepare a change. The live change is blocked until a human approves that exact change.

  • Step 1: Preview The assistant shows what it wants to change.
  • Step 2: Approve A manager approves the exact change.
  • Step 3: Apply The system checks that nothing changed, then applies or refuses.

What is blocked

Designed to avoid expensive mistakes.

1

No booking delete

The demo does not give agents a tool to delete or cancel reservations.

2

No hidden price change

Rate changes need a preview and a one-time approval before they go live.

3

No changed file trick

If the change is edited after approval, the live update is refused.

4

Extra care in production

Live hotel data needs extra confirmation before any write is allowed.

For managers

You do not need to understand the lock.

The important part is simple: the assistant can suggest. The manager decides. The system checks the approval before it touches live data.

Read-only by default

The assistant starts by looking up bookings, rooms, and rates.

Clear approval

Any live change has to be approved as a separate step.

Safe for demos

The demo works with sample data when no real credentials are present.
