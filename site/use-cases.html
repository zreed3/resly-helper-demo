Behind the scenes

A small team of helpers checks different parts of the hotel.

One helper checks bookings. One checks rooms. One checks prices. The manager gets one short answer, not a technical report.

Booking helper

Looks at arrivals, departures, changes, cancellations, and no-shows.

Room helper

Checks room types, occupancy, blocked rooms, and tight turnarounds.

Price helper

Reviews rates and prepares a change for approval when demand is high.

Can we take 3 people from July 5 to July 7?
Yes. Two room types fit 3 people. One Bedroom Garden is cheaper but tight on July 5.
What should I tell the guest?
Quote Two Bedroom Ocean if they want flexibility. Quote One Bedroom Garden only if they are ready to book now.

From a phone

Ask while you are away from the desk.

A manager can ask a simple question and get an answer based on room types, availability, and rates.

Ask:
"How many rooms are available for 3 people this weekend?"

Answer:
"Two options fit. One is cheaper. One has more availability."

CLI check:
resly --json availability quote --guests 3 --from 2026-07-05 --to 2026-07-07
