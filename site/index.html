The problem

Managers lose time chasing small answers.

A guest calls. Housekeeping needs priorities. Rates look wrong. A booking changed. The answer is usually in Resly, but staff still have to click around, compare screens, or ask someone else.

  • Less screen hopping Ask once and get the rooms, bookings, and rates checked together.
  • Less front-desk backtracking Staff can answer guests while the call is still happening.
  • Less pricing guesswork The assistant can prepare a rate change, but it cannot publish without approval.
